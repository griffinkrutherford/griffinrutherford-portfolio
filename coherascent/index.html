About Coherascent

Coherascent is a tech startup that combines a passion for mental and physical health with innovative software development. Our name is derived from "Coherent Ascension," reflecting our mission to help people meaningfully climb out of common societal health struggles.

We believe in a holistic approach that integrates well-being, fitness, mindful content consumption, nutrition, and positive outlook. Our solutions are designed to be accessible to people of all backgrounds and remain non-partisan in approach.

Our Approach

Mental Well-being

Personalized strategies to enhance mental clarity, reduce stress, and build resilience through evidence-based techniques.

Physical Fitness

Customized fitness plans that adapt to your progress, preferences, and goals for sustainable physical health.

Nutrition

Dietary guidance that works with your lifestyle, preferences, and nutritional needs for optimal health.

Tools to help you develop healthier relationships with digital content and technology.
